##  Overview
**AudioPlay** is a desktop music player that lets you play and manage your `audio` files easily.  
It features a minimal and intuitive interface ‚Äî just choose your music folder, reload, and enjoy.

1. **Open Settings**

2. **Save and Reload**

   The app will scan that folder and automatically list all the audio files.

3. **Play Music**
